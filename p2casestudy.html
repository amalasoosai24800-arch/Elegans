Oh Podu!

FMCG · Snacks & Packaged Foods | South India

A regional expression—rooted in sound, culture, and everyday emotion.

The Creative Process

From Concept Sketch to Brand Reality

Every impactful brand begins with a thought, a line, and a direction. This stage reflects our visual exploration and the foundation of the final identity.

The Question

The Question

How do you create a snack brand that feels instantly familiar—without leaning on nostalgia?

In a category crowded with memory-led storytelling, Oh Podu! needed a voice that felt current, culturally rooted, and scalable across regions—without losing its local soul.

The Insight

The Insight

Culture is not static. It speaks in tone, rhythm, and humour.

Across South India, expression lives in language, sound, and comic exaggeration. A brand that listens closely can feel native everywhere—without being generic anywhere.

The Folding

The Folding

We chose expression over sentimentality. Character over convention.

The strategy was to build a flexible identity system—one that could adapt to regional nuance while remaining unmistakably singular. A brand designed to entertain, not explain.

The Form

The Form

The brand took shape through a bold, cohesive system.
A distinctive logo anchored the identity. Packaging became the primary stage for storytelling. Mascots emerged as cultural carriers—each reflecting regional personality, humour, and tone.

A Brand Fun Book ensured consistency without restriction—guiding play, not limiting it.

The Unfolding

The Unfolding

Across four South Indian states, thirteen packaging variants came to life—each supported by region-specific mascots.
Four distinct characters allowed the brand to speak many dialects, while remaining recognisably Oh Podu! in spirit and form.

The result was a brand that could travel—without losing its voice.
